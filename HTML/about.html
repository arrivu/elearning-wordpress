About The Compelling Image

Welcome to The Compelling Image. We're an online and interactive school of photography and multimedia storytelling - all with an eye to visual expression in the digital age.

Learn online anywhere - anytime...

All of our courses take place in the internet. You won't need to schedule vacation time and travel long and expensive distances to enjoy and benefit from high-quality, interactive professional photography, multimedia and video instruction. All you need is a computer and a connection to the internet.

Getting started with a photography course

Just look through our exciting list of courses and portfolio reviews, read about our accomplished faculty of international instructors and click to enroll your choices. Using a major credit card or PayPal, you'll be guided through our secure payment process - that's it!

A "virtual classroom" experience

From your Student Area Dashboard, you'll be able to view analysis and constructive critiques of the assignment work you've submitted, as well as receive timely answers to questions posed to your instructor. You'll also be able to carry on lively discussions within your "classroom" - exchanging feedback and comments with others on your course. You can even schedule a Skype consultation with your instructor to discuss your progress.
